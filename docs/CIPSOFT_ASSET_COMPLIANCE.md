# CipSoft asset compliance register

Use this register before placing any Tibia-related media in a public release, runtime content pack, screenshot, video, website, or store listing. It records the source and allowed use of the asset group; it does not grant rights.

The official Fankit announcement requires users to read, understand, and accept the CipSoft Video and Screenshot Policy included in the Fankit. Keep a dated copy of the applicable policy with the private release records, then complete one entry per asset group.

Official references:

- <https://www.tibia.com/forum/?action=announcement&announcementid=87&boardid=89516>
- <https://static.tibia.com/download/TibiaFankit.zip>
- <https://www.tibia.com/abouttibia/?subtopic=aboutcipsoft>

## Required entry template

```text
Asset group:
Release/content-pack version:
Files or glob covered:
SHA-256 of final archive or inventory:
Source: Fankit / CipSoft policy permission / original project work / other (specify)
Official source URL:
Fankit archive version or retrieval date (if applicable):
Applicable policy name and date:
Permitted distribution and display scope:
Required acknowledgement placement:
Reviewer and review date:
Notes/exclusions:
```

## Decision rules

1. If a group is a direct match from the official Fankit, record its Fankit source and apply the included policy terms.
2. If a group was extracted from a Tibia client or another source, do not call it Fankit material unless there is a documented file-level or group-level match.
3. If the applicable terms allow display but not redistribution, keep the file out of downloadable content packs and use a permitted delivery or remove it.
4. Preserve the acknowledgement from [CREDITS.md](../CREDITS.md) wherever the reviewed material is publicly shown or distributed.
5. Re-review the register when the Fankit, policy, or content-pack inventory changes.
